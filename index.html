Quick Information

Hotel Samruddhi Garden is a F&B-Casual Dining outlet located in the heart of the city. It offers a wide variety of delicious cuisines from all over the world. The restaurant has a cozy and comfortable ambience with a pleasant atmosphere. The staff is friendly and attentive.
The menu includes a variety of dishes from Indian, Chinese, Continental and other cuisines. The restaurant also offers a variety of drinks and desserts. The prices are reasonable and the quality of food is excellent. The restaurant is a great place to enjoy a meal with family and friends. less
